Biography

I'm a researcher at IK4-IKERLAN. From 2015 to 2016, I worked at the Knowledge Media Institute, KMi (the Open University, UK). Before 2015, I worked at the MORElab research group and as an Assistant Professor at the University of Deusto. I finished a joint-degree on Computer Engineering and Engineering in Industrial Organization in 2008 and a MSc in Software Development in 2010 and my PhD in 2014 at theUniversity of Deusto.
