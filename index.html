About me

I'm a senior researcher at Tecnalia . From 2016 to 2019 I worked at Ikerlan and from 2015 to 2016, at the Knowledge Media Institute, KMi ( the Open University , UK) . Before 2015, I worked at the MORElab research group and as an Assistant Professor at the University of Deusto. I finished a joint-degree on Computer Engineering and Engineering in Industrial Organization in 2008 and a MSc in Software Development in 2010 and my PhD in 2014 at the University of Deusto.
